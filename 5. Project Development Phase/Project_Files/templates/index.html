About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is an Artificial Intelligence based Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine that recommends the best crop using soil nutrients and environmental conditions.

The system analyzes Nitrogen, Phosphorous, Potassium, Temperature, Humidity, pH and Rainfall. It helps farmers increase crop yield and make better agricultural decisions.
